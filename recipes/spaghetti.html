Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

Delicious and easy to make food

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • cheese

Steps to follow

  1. Cook spaghetti until al dente
  2. Sauté sliced garlic in olive oil until golden.
  3. Add red pepper flakes, then toss in the cooked spaghetti.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley and Parmesan, and serve.
  5. Cook fettuccine until al dente.
  6. In a pan, melt butter and sauté minced garlic.
  7. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  8. Stir in grated Parmesan until melted, then toss in the cooked fettuccine.
  9. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley, and serve.
