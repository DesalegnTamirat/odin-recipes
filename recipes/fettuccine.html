Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Lets prepare the dish you will remember forever

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley (optional, for garnish)

Preparation

  1. Cook the fettuccine in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat and saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the heavy cream to the pan and bring it to a gentle simmer.
  4. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and the sauce becomes smooth.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley, if desired, and serve immediately.
